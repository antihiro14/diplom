# Business Logic

The goal of the system is to analyze food from images.

## Workflow

1. Load image data.
2. Preprocess images.
3. Feed data into the model.
4. Train the model.
5. Predict food class.

## Limitations

- depends on dataset quality;
- early-stage accuracy may be low;
- complex images may cause errors.

## Future Development

- ingredient recognition;
- calorie estimation;
- real-time processing.
